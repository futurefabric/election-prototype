Governors' handbook

Detail

This departmental advice is for governing bodies in:

  • maintained schools
  • academies and free schools

The ‘Statutory policies for schools’ page outlines the policies and other documents school governing bodies are legally required to hold.

If you are a governor looking for more advice, you can contact GovernorLine free of charge.
