Policy

Universal Credit

From:
Department for Work and Pensions,The Rt Hon Iain Duncan Smith MP
Part of:
Benefits reform
Performance data:
Universal Credit performance

Universal Credit brings together 6 benefits for people who are out of work or on a low income into a single payment. It's being introduced in stages, starting in October 2013. By the end of 2017 it's expected to be available across England and Wales.
